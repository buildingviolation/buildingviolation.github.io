How does buildingviolation.com work for professionals?

how to help a customer with a building violation notice in 4 quick steps

1. We send you customer requests

  • Customers come to {{ site.domain }} looking to hire professionals to help them resolve notices of building violations
  • They fill a form that gathers specific information about their notices of building violation
  • The content of the form is submited and added to the pool of open cases

2. You decide who to respond to

  • You can browse the list of available cases
  • We send a notification to experienced professionals like you
  • If you are interested and qualified, you can respond by sending a quote

3. Send a quote

  • Your custom quote includes a personal message and a price estimate
  • We automatically send your buildingviolation.com profile containing past work, your qualifications, contact information, and reviews with your quote

4. Get hired, and let us know!

  • Typically many other professionals will also respond. The customer compares quotes and decides who to contact
  • You and the customer work out the details through buildingviolation.com, over the phone, or in person
  • When you're hired, please let us know and improve your chances of winning your next customer!

Professional Services

From small renovation jobs to full-scale property development, there are many regulations you need to follow for any construction project in your city. All of these projects require a permit expediter that can manage regulations, zoning rules and building codes quickly and efficiently.

To meet every permitting need, the building violation pros work closely with architects, engineers, plumbers and contractors to bring you the following services:

Permits and DOB Filing

The pros take care of anything you need done related to a permit application with your city's Department of Buildings (DOB).

  • They fill out all permit application forms and file them with the DOB on your behalf.
  • They guide you through the drafting process so your plans have the best chance of being approved on your first submittal to construction plan examiner in your city.
  • Once your permits are approved, they can also refer licensed and insured plumbers, contractors, and architects to you.
  • And when your project is completed, they close all job orders with the DOB in a legal and timely manner.

Building Violation Removal

There are many different types of violations property owners and managers can be charged with:

  • Housing Preservation and Development (HPD) Violations.
  • Environmental Control Board Violations.
  • Fire Department Violations.
  • Department of Transportation Violations.
  • Department of Sanitation Violations
  • Violations can happen to even the most careful new homeowners and seasoned real estate developers alike.

The pros can help you with violation removal in your city in various ways including representation at ECB court and on time filing of all paperwork to the Department of Buildings. However, the number one thing you can do to save yourself money and frustration is to avoid purchasing properties with violations in the first place.

Property Research

The Pros also offer property research services for prospective buyers and property owners and managers on all existing and prospective properties.
